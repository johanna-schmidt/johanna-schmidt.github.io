Welcome to our user study!

 

Thank you very much for participating.

Please only use the buttons for navigation.



Time required: The study will require about 25 minutes of your time.
Risks: There are no anticipated risks in this study.
Benefits: There are no direct benefits to you for participating in this research study.
Payment: You will receive no payment for participating in the interview.



Data recording

We will record the answers to the tasks and also record the time it took to complete the tasks. All data recording is done anonymous. The recorded data and the collected data from the anonymous questionnaires will be used for our research analysis. Use of this data will be strictly limited to this scientific project.

Privacy and anonymity

We will not ask for the name of the participants. Participants will only be represented by anonymous, automatically generated tokens. All data will be held in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and will not be used for any purpose for which it is not expressly permitted.

Your participation

Please note that you are free to participate in this study or not as you wish. You can withdraw your participation at any time without penalty. You can ask questions about the study at any time. You can ask for any material to be deleted or not to be used in any way.

How to withdraw from the study

If you want to withdraw from the study, just stop finishing the tasks and close your browser window. Incomplete material will not be stored and therefore not used in the evaluation of the study. If you would like to withdraw after your materials have been submitted, please contact Johanna Schmidt (johanna.schmidt@vrvis.at).
